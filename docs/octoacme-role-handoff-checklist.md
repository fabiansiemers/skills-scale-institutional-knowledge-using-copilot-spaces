## Purpose
Ensure smooth transitions when team members change roles, leave projects, or transfer responsibilities to minimize disruption and preserve institutional knowledge.

## When to use
- Team member is transitioning off the project
- Role responsibilities are being transferred to another person
- Planned leave of absence (extended vacation, parental leave, etc.)
- Organizational changes affecting project staffing

## Pre-Handoff Preparation

### Outgoing Team Member
- [ ] Notify Project Manager at least 2 weeks in advance (when possible)
- [ ] Document current work in progress with status updates
- [ ] Create list of key responsibilities and recurring tasks
- [ ] Identify critical knowledge areas unique to your role
- [ ] Prepare handoff documentation (see role-specific sections below)
- [ ] Clean up and organize workspace (files, notes, bookmarks)

### Project Manager
- [ ] Identify incoming team member or interim coverage
- [ ] Schedule handoff meetings and overlap time
- [ ] Update project staffing plan and stakeholder communications
- [ ] Review role responsibilities with both parties
- [ ] Ensure access and permissions are ready for incoming member

## General Handoff Checklist

### Knowledge Transfer
- [ ] Schedule at least one extended handoff session (1-2 hours)
- [ ] Review current sprint/iteration status and priorities
- [ ] Walk through work in progress and next steps
- [ ] Share context on recent decisions and their rationale
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders and their expectations
- [ ] Review communication cadence and reporting requirements
- [ ] Discuss known issues, risks, and mitigation plans

### Documentation
- [ ] Update project documentation with current state
- [ ] Document passwords, access credentials (via secure channel)
- [ ] Share important links, tools, and resources
- [ ] Transfer ownership of key documents and artifacts
- [ ] Update team contact lists and org charts
- [ ] Archive or organize email threads and chat channels

### Access & Tools
- [ ] Transfer or grant access to necessary tools and systems
- [ ] Update permissions in repositories, project boards, and platforms
- [ ] Share calendar invites for recurring meetings
- [ ] Update notification settings and subscriptions
- [ ] Transfer ownership of tasks and issues
- [ ] Update team distribution lists and contact info

### Stakeholder Communication
- [ ] Announce transition to relevant stakeholders
- [ ] Introduce incoming team member to key contacts
- [ ] Clarify interim points of contact during transition
- [ ] Update out-of-office messages and email signatures

## Role-Specific Handoff

### Developers
- [ ] Review active branches and PRs with context
- [ ] Document local development setup and configurations
- [ ] Share knowledge of complex codebases or modules
- [ ] Explain technical decisions and architecture choices
- [ ] Transfer debugging tips and known workarounds
- [ ] Review test coverage gaps and technical debt items
- [ ] Share performance optimization insights

### Product Managers
- [ ] Transfer product roadmap and backlog ownership
- [ ] Share customer feedback and research insights
- [ ] Review prioritization rationale for key features
- [ ] Introduce to key customers and stakeholders
- [ ] Transfer ownership of OKRs and success metrics
- [ ] Share competitive analysis and market insights
- [ ] Review upcoming product launches and timelines

### Project Managers
- [ ] Transfer project charter and planning documents
- [ ] Review risk register and mitigation strategies
- [ ] Share vendor and contractor relationships
- [ ] Transfer budget tracking and resource allocation
- [ ] Review dependency management and coordination points
- [ ] Share stakeholder management insights
- [ ] Transfer status reporting templates and cadence

### UX Designers
- [ ] Transfer design files and source files
- [ ] Share design system updates and component status
- [ ] Review user research findings and insights
- [ ] Transfer ongoing usability test plans
- [ ] Share accessibility audit results
- [ ] Review design debt and improvement backlog
- [ ] Introduce to design tool administrators

### Scrum Masters / Agile Coaches
- [ ] Share team health observations and metrics
- [ ] Review retrospective action items and progress
- [ ] Transfer facilitation notes and meeting templates
- [ ] Share team dynamics and individual coaching notes (as appropriate)
- [ ] Review impediments and escalation history
- [ ] Share agile maturity assessment insights
- [ ] Transfer ceremony schedules and formats

### Technical Architects
- [ ] Transfer architecture documentation and ADRs
- [ ] Share technical vision and strategic direction
- [ ] Review architectural debt and refactoring priorities
- [ ] Transfer technology evaluation in progress
- [ ] Share security and compliance considerations
- [ ] Review technical standards and guidelines
- [ ] Introduce to architecture review board

### Customer/End-User Advocates
- [ ] Transfer user feedback database and analysis
- [ ] Share top user pain points and feature requests
- [ ] Review user persona insights and updates
- [ ] Transfer relationships with customer advisory board
- [ ] Share user interview schedules and contacts
- [ ] Review feature validation results
- [ ] Transfer user community management responsibilities

### Data Analysts
- [ ] Transfer dashboard ownership and data sources
- [ ] Share SQL queries and analysis scripts
- [ ] Review KPI definitions and measurement approaches
- [ ] Transfer experiment tracking and results
- [ ] Share data quality issues and workarounds
- [ ] Review reporting schedules and stakeholders
- [ ] Transfer access to analytics platforms

## Handoff Verification

### Within First Week
- [ ] Incoming member can access all necessary systems
- [ ] Incoming member has attended key recurring meetings
- [ ] Incoming member has met with critical stakeholders
- [ ] Incoming member understands immediate priorities

### Within First Month
- [ ] Incoming member is independently completing core tasks
- [ ] Outgoing member is available for questions (if possible)
- [ ] Project Manager confirms successful transition
- [ ] No critical knowledge gaps have been identified

## Post-Handoff

### Outgoing Team Member
- [ ] Remain available for questions during transition period (typically 1-2 weeks)
- [ ] Provide contact info for follow-up questions (if appropriate)
- [ ] Complete exit survey or feedback session

### Incoming Team Member
- [ ] Document gaps or unclear areas discovered during handoff
- [ ] Update handoff documentation based on experience
- [ ] Share feedback to improve future handoffs

### Project Manager
- [ ] Monitor transition and address any gaps quickly
- [ ] Update team roster and stakeholder communications
- [ ] Conduct retrospective on handoff process
- [ ] Update handoff checklist based on learnings

## Emergency Handoff (Unplanned Departures)

When a handoff must happen quickly or without the outgoing member's participation:
- [ ] Identify team members who can provide context
- [ ] Review recent commits, PRs, documents, and communications
- [ ] Schedule knowledge-sharing sessions with teammates
- [ ] Document unknowns and risks for stakeholder awareness
- [ ] Establish interim coverage while ramping up
- [ ] Plan for longer transition period and gradual assumption of responsibilities

## Continuous Improvement
- Gather feedback from both outgoing and incoming team members
- Update this checklist based on handoff experiences
- Share learnings in retrospectives to improve the process
